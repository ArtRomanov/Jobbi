# Jobbi — Product Summary

**Vision:** A friendly, all-in-one personal assistant that organizes the entire job search — from research to offer — in one place, replacing scattered spreadsheets and docs with a clear, efficient workflow.

**Target Audience:** Tech professional actively job hunting who needs a centralized personal tool to manage the full job search lifecycle.

**Core Features:**

- **Application Tracker** — Kanban/timeline board to manage applications through stages (Researching, Applied, Interview, Offer, Rejected).
- **CV Constructor** — Build, store, and tailor multiple CV versions per role.
- **AI Chat Integration** — AI assistant for cover letters, CV refinement, and interview prep.
- **Metrics Dashboard** — Visual stats on applications, response rates, and pipeline health.
